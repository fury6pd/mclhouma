    Our Mission

  • The Houma-Terrebonne Marine Corps League has dedicated itself to our military brothers and sisters, past and present, as well as serving our community in varies ways.

  • The mission of the Marine Corps League is to promote the interest and to preserve traditions of the United States Marine Corps; strengthen the fraternity of Marines and their families; serve Marines and FMF Navy Personnel who wear or who have worn the Eagle, Globe and Anchor; and foster the ideals of Americanism and patriotic volunteerism.

    • WHAT WE DO

    Military Funeral Honors

    It is our honor and duty to pay final respects to milirary personnel of all branches who have served our country honorably. Our services include a 21-gun volley and playing of TAPS. We also often request an opportunity to perform a "Pass and Review" in which we take a moment to pay our final respects to our deceased brother or sister with a final salute while our bugler performs "Battle Hymn of the Republic/Glory Halelujah."

    Flag-Raising Ceremonies

    When requested, we honor our nation's flag by hoisting it in conjuction with the playing of "To The Colors", followed by our National Anthem.

    Supporting Our Community

    Throughout the year, we are priviledged to support and participate in events and fundraisers hosted by various other organizations.
